Nijue Smart Business Card

Nijue, a cutting-edge NFC-based virtual business card, embodies its essence in its name derived from the Swahili word–Nijue– which means, "Get to Know Me." This core principle underpins Nijue's mission to revolutionize the way professionals showcase their information and connect with others. By seamlessly integrating the power of NFC technology and QR codes, Nijue offers a dynamic and customizable online portfolio accessible through a simple tap or scan, facilitating an impressive and engaging introduction in the modern professional landscape.

How it Works:

  1. Ordering the Card: Individuals can order their Nijue virtual business card, specifying their name, designation, and optionally their company's logo for printing.
  2. Front of the Card: The front of the card showcases the person's name, designation, and if requested, their company's logo.
  3. Back of the Card: The back of the card features an NFC chip and a QR code linked to a unique URL on nijue.co.ke.
  4. Accessing the Portfolio: When someone taps the card with their phone or scans the QR code, it directs them to a personalized registration page on nijue.co.ke.
  5. Profile Registration: The registration page allows users to create their profile by uploading a profile picture, editing social media handles, adding contact details (phone number, email), writing a personal description, and including any relevant links.
  6. Saving and Editing: Users save their information, creating a personalized online portfolio. They can log in at any time to edit or update their details, including new job titles or promotions.
  7. Online Portfolio: The online portfolio showcases all the information provided, offering a comprehensive view of the individual's professional identity and contacts.
  8. Easy Updates: If there are changes in their profile, users can simply log in, edit the necessary details, and instantly update their online portfolio.

This innovative solution eliminates the need for traditional business cards and allows professionals to maintain an up-to-date and easily accessible profile, enhancing networking and connectivity.
